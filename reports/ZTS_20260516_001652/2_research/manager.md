**Recommendation**: Underweight

**Rationale**: For ZTS, the bull made the stronger case that the *business* remains high quality (elite margins, cash generation, leadership in animal health) and that a post-earnings drawdown can create opportunity. However, the bear’s case is stronger on *why this is not the right time to add risk*: the latest quarter/guidance imply a clear growth reset (low-single-digit reported growth, flat organic growth, guidance below expectations), while the stock is in a severe technical/positioning breakdown (heavy-volume gap down, deeply negative momentum). In that setup, valuation alone is not a catalyst—if estimates are still drifting lower, the “cheap” multiple can prove illusory. Net: ZTS may be a good company, but it is currently a poor entry point until either (a) fundamentals re-accelerate or (b) the tape repairs and confirms stabilization.
